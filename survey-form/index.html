COVID-19 Survey

The purpose of this survey is to gather information on the prevalence of Covid-19 and people's awareness of the disease. Results from this survey will only be used for academic research.

Participation is voluntary. However, you have to be at least 18 years old in order to participate.

Questions marked by (*) are required and must be completed before the form can be submitted.

Thank you for your time and participation.
